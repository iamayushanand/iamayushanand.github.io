Motive

This websites aimes to help students of fiitjee ameerpet resolve their doubts through open collaboration.
Students can post their doubts or browse through the catalog to see if their problem is already solved by their peers.

How To Use This Website?

To use this website students need to follow certain steps .
1) to see solutions for a chapter in the book deepti maths just go to http://iamayushanand.github.io/maths/deepti/{chapter number}
2) to see solutions for a chapter in the book 38 years by arihant just go to http://iamayushanand.github.io/maths/38yrs/{chapter number}
3) to see solutins for a chapter in compendium just go to http://iamayushanand.github.io/maths/compendium/{chapter number}
Note: You can contribute to us by posting pictures of your reference books so that other students can use them.
